CLIMBING EXPERIENCE

Auto belays allow individual
climbers to climb the wall and come
back to the ground on their own.
The tethers on these devices retract
as the climber moves up the wall.
When the climber lets go of the
wall, the device lowers them to the
ground at a consistent speed.

BOULDERING

Bouldering involves climbing lower
to the ground without using the
ropes and therefore, the only
equipment required is climbing
shoes and chalk. It is a great way
to learn and practice movement
techniques and challenge yourself
on ‘problems’ (designated paths on
the wall specifically for
bouldering).

KIDS CLIMBING
ADVENTURE

The Kids Climbing Adventure is a
drop-in program allowing your kids
a full day of climbing fun! At this
age, participants must be
accompanied by a parent or adult
who is on the floor to supervise and
to hold the ropes for the kids while
they climb. The belayer does not
have to climb.
